Our approach


In today's fast-paced technology landscape, finding the right talent quickly and efficiently is crucial for meeting client needs. At Relevance Infosystems, we have developed a unique staffing approach that combines traditional recruitment methods with a proactive, network-driven strategy. Over the years, we have built a robust database of potential technology consultants, meticulously curated through our technology experts and our Technology Network Group.

Building a Specialized Database

Our staffing process begins with the establishment of a comprehensive database that houses a diverse range of technology consultants. This database is enriched through ongoing interactions with our technology experts, who identify and engage with skilled professionals in various domains. By leveraging our Technology Network Group, we tap into a vast pool of talent that extends beyond conventional job portals and social media platforms. This enables us to source consultants with niche skills and extensive experience, ensuring that we can meet the specific needs of our clients.

Understanding Client Requirements

A cornerstone of our effective staffing approach is our deep understanding of client requirements and the specific projects consultants will be working on. We engage in thorough discussions with clients to grasp their objectives, project scopes, and technical needs. This insight allows us to identify the right consultants who not only possess the required skills but also align well with the project dynamics.

Involving Technology and Domain Experts

To ensure the highest quality in consultant selection, we involve our technology and domain experts in the selection process. These experts have hands-on experience in the industry and a comprehensive understanding of various technologies and project methodologies. Their expertise allows us to evaluate candidates not just on paper, but also in terms of their practical capabilities and how they can contribute to the success of specific projects. This collaborative approach enhances the precision of our consultant matching, leading to better outcomes for our clients.

Rapid Identification and Deployment of Consultants

One of the key advantages of our approach is the speed with which we can identify and deploy qualified consultants. Our technology experts are well-versed in their respective areas, allowing them to quickly assess project requirements and match them with appropriate talent from our database. Moreover, we maintain a continuous dialogue with our consultants to keep track of their availability and current engagement statuses. This rapport ensures that we can deploy consultants who are ready to join projects immediately, minimizing downtime for our clients.

Facilitating Client Interviews

Once we identify suitable consultants, we facilitate prompt introductions and interviews between our clients and the candidates. Our technology experts play a crucial role in this stage as well; they provide insights into each consultant's technical expertise and project experience, ensuring that clients have a clear understanding of the value each candidate brings to the table. This collaborative approach not only enhances the interview process but also fosters a stronger connection between the consultants and clients.


By combining our extensive database of technology consultants with a keen understanding of client needs, the expertise of our technology and domain specialists, and a commitment to maintaining strong relationships with our consultants, we have created a staffing approach that is both efficient and effective. Our ability to quickly identify and deploy qualified consultants ensures that we meet client demands with agility and precision. At Relevance Infosystems, we are committed to delivering exceptional talent solutions that empower our clients to achieve their technology goals.
